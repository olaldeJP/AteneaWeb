History and Vision

We are a consulting firm dedicated to efficiently and transparently providing high quality, integrated services to governments, public agencies and the private sector. We offer innovative and effective solutions in advising, designing, implementing, evaluating and managing the challenges faced by both sectors. We pride ourselves on our ability to manage and deliver projects of varying scale and complexity.

We support public and private organisations in the management and implementation of development and growth projects, committed to the highest international standards of environmental sustainability, in line with global agreements such as the Paris Agreement and the Tokyo Protocol. These commitments reflect our support for the 2030 Agenda and the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). We strive to have a positive impact on society and the environment, promoting projects that foster equitable, responsible and environmentally friendly growth.

Culture and Values

At Atenea Advice, we are fully committed to our clients, striving to exceed their expectations in every strategy we undertake. We are guided by fundamental principles: transparency, work ethics, integrity, dedication to excellence and corporate social responsibility. We promote the protection of the environment and the well-being of communities, with the firm conviction that sustainable growth is the way to achieve it. These convictions are the pillars that underpin every action we take and reflect our identity as an organisation.

Services

We focus on collaborative work with our clients, understanding their specific needs and offering solutions adapted to their particular context, which allows us to propose a broad and adequate perspective to each project, convinced that with high quality management it is possible to reach the desired goal. the desired objective.

Management of international financing

Our services include trade finance advisory, international loan structuring, foreign exchange risk management and tailor-made credit facilities. With a global presence and a team of international finance experts, Atenea provides you with the tools you need to successfully navigate the complex global financial landscape.

Strategic planning

We support governments and organisations in defining their objectives through the development of strategic plans. This service involves the identification of strengths and weaknesses of the organisation, the assessment of risks and opportunities, the design of monitoring indicators and the definition of specific action plans.

Project management

We provide project management services, focusing on the definition of objectives and scope, planning and monitoring of activities, management of procurement and contracting processes, management of human and financial resources, identification of risks and preparation of monitoring reports.

Evaluation and Programme Formulation

We carry out evaluations of public programmes and projects (funded from local sources or by WTO), with the aim of measuring their impact and effectiveness, among other possible evaluation criteria. This service includes the definition of evaluation indicators, data collection and analysis, the preparation of evaluation reports and the recommendation of improvement actions.

Training and education

We offer training and capacity building servicesb to the human resources of governments and public organisations in various areas, such as strategic planning, project management, evaluation of public programmes and policies, project management with WTO, among others. This service includes the development of training materials and the delivery of courses and workshops.

Public policy advice and technical assistance

We advise governments (national, provincial and municipal) and public organisations on the formulation, design, implementation and evaluation of public policies in various areas, such as health, education, security, environment, financial and tax administration, among others. This service includes data analysis, identification of trends and international best practices, elaboration of strategies and action plans, impact assessment and measurement of results. Technical advice and assistance includes projects implemented with funds from Multilateral Credit Organisations (MCOs).
